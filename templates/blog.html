Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

Leveraging Flask & Machine Learning

The Problem

Many people struggle to differentiate between diseases due to overlapping symptoms. Our goal was to provide a quick and accurate way for users to gain insights into their health.

The Solution

We created a web app where users enter their symptoms, and a trained Decision Tree Classifier model predicts the most likely disease. The app provides valuable information on:

  • Symptom Input: A simple, user-friendly form to enter symptoms.
  • Machine Learning Model: A trained Decision Tree Classifier for accurate disease predictions.
  • Detailed Insights: Descriptions, precautions, medications, and lifestyle recommendations.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy navigation for all users.
  • Accurate Predictions: AI-driven results for better health awareness.
  • Comprehensive Health Tips: Diet, medication, and workout recommendations.
  • Educational Content: Informative blog posts about various health topics.

Conclusion

This project bridges the gap between technology and healthcare, offering a fast and reliable solution for users worldwide. By integrating AI into healthcare, we empower individuals with accessible and valuable health information.
